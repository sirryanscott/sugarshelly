About

Hello and welcome to my bakery, a home based, licensed business that offers a variety of uniquely designed baked goodies that are made to order and delivered directly to you.

My experience of baking spans over 25 years for family and friends, but it has been my desire for some time to take it to the next level and open a bakery.

The name of my bakery is in loving memory of my daughter Cassie (aka "Spoon") who has taught me to "live by faith not fear". A portion of the proceeds will be donated to charity in honor of her.

It would be my pleasure to help you create a delicious and unique dessert for your next event.

Thank you,
